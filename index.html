About Us

Our journey of storytelling and impact

Our Story

Justice Baidoo Consulting Limited is a pan-African documentary production, communications and knowledge management firm based in Accra, Ghana.

We have over a decade of frontline journalism and documentary filmmaking experience working and collaborating with top institutions like the BBC, France 24, UNICEF, UNDP, GIZ, Solidaridad, Plan International and SEND West Africa, among others.
